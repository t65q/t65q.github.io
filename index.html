About

Troop 65 is a Boy Scouts of America troop located in Astoria, New York.

We meet weekly on Wednesday nights at Variety Boys and Girls Club. Our meeting times are from 7:00 to 8:30 PM.

Our Scouts plan monthly outings that are fun and that teach lifelong lessons. Examples include camping, hiking, orienteering, and more. If you're looking for a Troop, come check us out!
